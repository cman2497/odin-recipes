Homemade Cheesecake

Description

A homemade cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made by combining cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and other ingredients, and baking them in a crust made from crushed cookies or graham crackers. It is typically served chilled and topped with fruit, chocolate sauce, or other desired toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of crushed graham crackers or digestive biscuits
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 3 (8 oz) packages of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream or heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

How to make dish:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the crushed graham crackers or digestive biscuits and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese with an electric mixer until it is smooth and creamy. Gradually add the sugar and beat until well combined.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the sour cream or heavy cream and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the cheesecake batter into the prepared crust, smoothing the top with a spatula.
  6. Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for about 45-55 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is golden brown.
  7. Let the cheesecake cool to room temperature, then chill it in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
  8. Serve the cheesecake chilled, topped with your choice of fruit, chocolate sauce, or other desired toppings. Enjoy!
