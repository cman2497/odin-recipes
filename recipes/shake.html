Banana Milkshake

Description

A banana milkshake is a cold and creamy drink made by blending bananas, milk, and ice cream together until smooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of vanilla ice cream

How to make dish:

  1. Peel the bananas and cut them into small pieces. Place the banana pieces in a blender.
  2. Add the milk and ice cream to the blender and blend on high speed until the ingredients are smooth and well combined.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass and serve immediately. You can garnish the milkshake with whipped cream, a cherry, or additional sliced bananas, if desired.
