Homemade Pozole

Description

Pozole is a traditional Mexican soup or stew made with hominy and pork or chicken. It is often served with a variety of garnishes, including shredded lettuce, diced onions, sliced radishes, avocado, and lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • Hominy
  • Pork or chicken
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spices (such as cumin, oregano, and chili powder)
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sliced radishes
  • Lime wedges (for garnish)
  • Corn tortillas (for serving)

How to make dish:

  1. Begin by soaking the hominy in water for at least 6 hours or overnight. Drain the hominy and set it aside.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium-high heat. Add the pork or chicken and cook until it is browned on all sides.
  3. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and saut√© until they are softened.
  4. Add the drained hominy to the pot, along with the spices and enough water to cover the ingredients by about 2 inches.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 2 hours, or until the pork or chicken is tender.
  6. Once the soup is done, taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Serve the soup hot, garnished with shredded cabbage, diced tomatoes, sliced radishes, avocado, and lime wedges, if desired. Serve with corn tortillas on the side.
